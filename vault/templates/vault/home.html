How To Use

Create your account

Create an account by entering your email address and setting a Master Password. Your data is stored in your account. A Master Password is the only password you need to remember.

Add your passwords

Add your passwords manually. You can easily store unlimited number of passwords.

Access your passwords

You can easily access all the passwords you stored just by having access to internet. Our web based password manager is accessible on all major browsers, meaning you'll find your passwords on all your devices.
